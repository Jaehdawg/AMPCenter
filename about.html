Battery Innovation and Pilot Line Access / Power and Grid Resilience Research

BATTERY INNOVATION and PILOT LINE ACCESS.

AMP’s Battery Research Center supports the full lifecycle of battery development — from material evaluation and cell design to manufacturing scale-up and commercialization readiness.

By combining applied research with pilot-scale production capabilities, AMP helps industry partners reduce technical risk, validate processes, and accelerate time to market. Located within the Battery Research Center, these capabilities provide a unique environment to validate technologies, test materials, and scale manufacturing processes.

POWER and GRID RESILIENCE RESEARCH.

As electrification accelerates and energy demand grows, resilient and adaptive power systems are essential.

AMP’s power research lab enables utilities, manufacturers, and technology providers to model, test, and validate solutions that improve reliability, optimize energy use, and support emerging high-load applications. Through advanced simulation and real-world testing, partners can evaluate next-generation energy systems and infrastructure in a controlled, collaborative environment.
