Advisory board

Strategic experts guiding battery, energy, and commercialization direction.

Bob Galyen

Advisory board

Bob Galyen is a globally recognized battery expert with nearly 50 years of experience in the automotive and energy storage industries. He served in leadership roles at GM, Delphi, Magna, and as CTO of CATL, the world’s largest EV battery manufacturer. Today he leads Galyen Energy, serves on multiple corporate and advisory boards, and is CTO of NAATBatt International.

Dr. James Trevey

Advisory board

Dr. James Trevey is CTO of Forge Nano and an expert in lithium-ion batteries, materials science, and technology scaling. He is President-Elect of NAATBatt, Founder and Vice President of the Military Power Sources Consortium, and Industry Lead for Li-Bridge, advancing domestic energy security through collaboration across industry and government.

Dr. John Warner

Advisory board

Dr. John Warner is Chief Customer Officer at American Battery Solutions and President of Warner Energy Consulting LLC. With over 30 years in the automotive and battery industries, he has held executive roles at EnerDel, XALT Energy, Magna Steyr, Boston-Power, and General Motors. He is a Chairman Emeritus of NAATBatt International, serves on SAE battery standards committees, and is the author of two widely used books on lithium-ion batteries.

Shawn Murphy

Advisory board

Shawn Murphy is Co-founder, CEO, and CTO of Titan Advanced Energy Solutions. An entrepreneur and technologist with more than 25 years of experience, he has founded multiple startups and led innovation programs in aerospace, defense, semiconductors, oil and gas, and energy systems. Previously, he directed Earth and Space Science at Draper Laboratory and launched Shell TechWorks, a technology commercialization center for Royal Dutch Shell.

Dr. Alec Falzone

Advisory board

Dr. Alec Falzone is Growth Leader for Battery Manufacturing at Honeywell. He has built his career in the lithium-ion battery industry across both technical and commercial roles, applying advanced measurement, digitization, and process control solutions to battery manufacturing. He holds a Ph.D. in Inorganic Chemistry from North Carolina State University.

Lucas (Luke) Wells

Advisory board

Luke Wells is a Scientist at BI by UL Solutions in Aachen, Germany, with more than 15 years of experience in engineering, chemical engineering, and materials science. He leads projects on next-generation lithium-ion cell manufacturing, failure analysis, and lab growth strategy. Luke is also founder of Energy Wells, a consultancy focused on scaling lithium-ion cell production and optimizing manufacturing efficiency.

Michael Malley

Advisory board / AMP Center

Michael Malley serves as Manager of Strategic Research Partnerships at the AMP Center within the Alabama Transportation Institute. He leads the implementation of both the Battery Research Center and Power Research Center and has been deeply involved in AMP’s strategic mission spanning use-inspired research, economic development, and workforce development.

Barry May

Workforce development

Dana Bubonovich

Workforce development

Ajay Gnanasekaran

Workforce development

He brings deep expertise in battery technology, manufacturing, and workforce strategy to support the growing demands of the US EV industry.

Renu Dalal

Workforce development

She works with industry, education, and workforce partners to design training programs that align with the needs of battery manufacturing, electrification, and advanced power systems.
