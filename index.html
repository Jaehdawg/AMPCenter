About AMP

The AMP Center brings together industry, researchers, and workforce partners to accelerate innovation in battery manufacturing, power and grid resilience, charging infrastructure, and electrification.

With more than 50 industry and strategic partners engaged through its consortium, the AMP Center fosters collaboration that connects research, commercialization, and workforce development.
