Power Research Lab

Power Research Lab

Grid simulation and resilience testing. Electrification and charging infrastructure research. Smart infrastructure and connected energy systems.

GRID SIMULATION and RESILIENCE TESTING.

The lab allows partners to model and evaluate how energy systems respond to changing grid conditions and disruptions.

Capabilities include grid resiliency and outage recovery scenarios, load growth and high-demand infrastructure planning, demand shifting and load management strategies, distributed energy resource (DER) integration, energy storage system integration, and microgrid and grid-edge system evaluation.

INTEGRATED ENERGY SYSTEMS INNOVATION.

By combining grid simulation, electrification research, and advanced communications infrastructure, the AMP Power Research Lab provides a comprehensive platform to evaluate how next-generation energy systems perform in real-world conditions.

5G ORAN INNOVATION TESTBED

AMP operates a private 5G ORAN (Open Radio Access Network) testbed that enables secure, real-time communication across connected energy and mobility systems.

POWER RESEARCH LAB AT A GLANCE

~10,000 sq ft indoor lab with outdoor equipment pad. 2,000 A, 480 V, 3-phase primary service. 1,100 VDC SPOC DC Power Grid system. 2 MW AC inverter capacity. 1 MW DC-DC conversion capacity. 1.25 MW diesel generator. 250 kWh current battery capacity, expandable to ~1.5 MWh. 30 kW rooftop solar PV array. 12 charging stations. Private 5G ORAN network. PLC, SCADA, HMI, and advanced controls.
